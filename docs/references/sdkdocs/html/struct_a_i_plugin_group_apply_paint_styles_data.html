AIBoolean AIPluginGroupApplyPaintStylesData::applyStyleTransform

When true, apply a transformation using matrix, transformFlags, and transformLineScale, and ignore pathStyle and pathStyleMap.


When false, merge styles using pathStyle and pathStyleMap, and ignore matrix, transformFlags, and transformLineScale.

long AIPluginGroupApplyPaintStylesData::transformFlags

Transformation options, a logical OR of AITransformArtOptions.

kTransformObjects and kTransformChildren are always off, and at least one of kTransformFillGradients, kTransformFillPatterns, kTransformStrokePatterns, or kScaleLines is on.
